Side B — Track 3

Looking Back

If I could give advice to my first-year self, what would it be?

I would tell my first-year self to start building projects earlier, ask more questions and not be afraid of difficult concepts. Growth in technology comes from consistent practice, patience and learning through mistakes, not from waiting until everything feels perfect. I would also remind myself to document my work well, stay curious and treat every project as an opportunity to improve both technical and communication skills.
